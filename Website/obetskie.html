Etymology

One of the most notable features of jeepneys is their extended chassis, which allows for increased passenger capacity. This extension is often complemented by metal roofs and open sides, providing ventilation in the tropical climate. Inside, the seating arrangement typically consists of two long benches facing each other, maximizing space and fostering a communal atmosphere among passengers. Despite their utilitarian origins, jeepneys are equipped with various amenities such as padded seats, decorative lights, and even sound systems, enhancing the travel experience
