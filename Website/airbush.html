Airbrush drawing

Colorful stickers typically decorate a jeep’s body. But a patók uses acrylic and enamel paints, sprayed with airbrushes. According to a patók designer/painter, using custom-made stickers is more expensive and timeconsuming. Thus, new patók owners prefer airbrush-painted designs, so that they can complete rush orders and save production costs. Though cheaper, these designs are not necessarily inferior because the outcome still depends on the creativity of the designer.
