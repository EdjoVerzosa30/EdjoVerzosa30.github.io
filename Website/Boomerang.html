Boomerang

The boomerang, as drivers call it, helps passengers identify an incoming patók.It is placed prominently on the roof towards the front. Made of stainless steel, the boomerang is positioned like the letter V and resembles the horn of the Philippine national animal, the water buffalo (carabao).
